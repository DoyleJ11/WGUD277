About Bainbridge Island

Bainbridge Island is a city in Kitsap County, Washington. It was incorporated in 1947 and is located in the Puget Sound region.

  • Population: 24,846
  • Year Incorporated: 1947
  • Region: Puget Sound
  • Classification: Suburban
  • Average Income Level: High
