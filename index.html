About Washington State

Washington, the Evergreen State, is known for its stunning natural beauty and vibrant cities. Explore the capital, Olympia, the charming Bainbridge Island, and the bustling city of Seattle.

Intersting Facts

  • Mount Rainier is the highest peak in Washington.
  • Washington is home to the famous Space Needle in Seattle.
  • Washington produces more apples than any other state.
